About me

Mara is an enthusiastic digital transformation veteran with 18+ years in the software industry.

An experienced manager and effective communicator in English, Portuguese and Spanish, Mara has succesfully led businesses and sales teams in Latin America and Asia Pacific. She is comfortable working in a challenging, fast-paced and changing environment fueled by a passion for technology, coffee and places (not necessarily in that order) along with a personal long term commitment to ensure children and youth thrives through science, innovation and leadership by engaging, throughout her career, in volunteer initiatives in IBM and in AIESEC.

Although she has a solid skill set in web technologies, middleware and mainframe software, Mara is relentelesly curious about tech trends and has been an active member of the Blockchain, DevOps, IoT and Machine Learning MeetUp communities in London.

She resides in London and has a BA in Communications from Fundacao Armando Alvares Penteado in Brazil, a MSc in Leadership and Strategy from London Business School in UK and is certified in Digital Marketing by IAB Europe.
